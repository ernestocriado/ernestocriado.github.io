At Caltech, a major goal of my research has been to develop tools for remotely interacting with living systems. This includes sonogenetic control of cellular function, ultrasound neuromodulation strategies, and collaborations on implantable piezoelectric systems for deep brain activation.

The unifying objective is to create non-invasive or minimally invasive interfaces that can perturb biological function with improved specificity and translational potential.
