This was a short but really fun collaboration!

My labmate Ann Liu asked if I could help her model how a subQ tumor heats up under a therapeutic ultrasound transducer using a hydrophone-measured acoustic field. We put together an axisymmetric bioheat FEM model with a PID control loop informed by IR surface imaging, and the results were super interesting.

The tissue warms up and quickly homogenizes, with no long-lasting hotspots inside the tumor.
